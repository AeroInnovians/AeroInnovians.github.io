At our company, we are committed to revolutionizing waste management and air purification through innovative, eco-friendly technologies.

Our mission is to promote sustainability and self-sufficiency, creating solutions that not only tackle environmental challenges but also improve the quality of life for communities globally.

  • Our flagship innovation focuses on developing a device that transforms burning parali (rice straw) into fertilizers and electricity.

    This initiative addresses both air pollution and energy shortages in rural areas.

  • Our cutting-edge product aims to reduce environmental impact, support renewable energy initiatives, and contribute to a healthier planet.

    We envision a world where clean air is accessible to everyone, waste is effectively managed, and sustainable practices are woven into daily life.

The project encompasses designing, prototyping, and testing this technology, followed by community training for local farmers. Ultimately, we aspire to turn agricultural waste into a sustainable source of nitrous fertilizers and energy, fostering environmental health and economic benefits for rural communities. Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, more sustainable future.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work without electricity?

The thermal energy generated is enough for upflow of air through the filters. For extra efficiency, manual methods can be integrated into the contraption.

Is it safe for indoor use?

Yes, AeroGen AIR is designed with advanced filteration and controlled combustion to ensure safe and clean output.

What is it made of?

AeroGen AIR is made out of sustainable, eco-friendly materials for environmental protection and is engineered for utmost durability.

Is it suitable for all environments?

Yes, AeroGen AIR is adaptable for both residential and industrial spaces, especially in areas with air quality concerns.

How efficient is the residual fertilizer for crops?

It is considerably effective for crops due to its high nitrogenous content and long lasting effect. Note: fertilizer to be used in moderation as per guidelines so as to ensure prevention of loss of natural soil fertility.

How much time can it function without stopping?

It can function effortlessly for 2 hours on automatic mode and 1 hour on manual mode.
