What is BreatheGuard?

BreatheGuard is an innovative web-based application by AeroInnovians that empowers users to check air pollution levels in any city using real-time AQI data. Based on air quality, it offers science-backed health advice tailored to the pollution severity.

  • Real-time air quality data (via IQAir).
  • Actionable health recommendations (via Gemini AI).
  • Designed for global cities.

Why Choose BreatheGuard?

Harnessing the power of real-time data and AI to bring you actionable health insights.

Live AQI Monitoring

Tracks real-time air quality for your city, focusing on the most harmful pollutants like PM2.5, directly from IQAir.

Personalized Health Tips

Provides AI-generated recommendations based on live AQI levels to help you minimize health risks.

Mobile-Optimized UI

A lightweight and fast interface ensures you can get information quickly, even in low-connectivity areas.

Live Air Quality Check

Enter a city, state, and country to get the latest PM2.5 air quality data and personalized health suggestions.
